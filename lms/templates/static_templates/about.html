${_('About')}

We, Dr Lesley Gardner, Dr Don Sheridan, and the PhD student, Andy Nguyen, are researchers at the University of Auckland Business School.

AucklandInsights aims to propose and implement conceptual and system artefacts to apply learning analytics in the Higher Education domain.

This project also seeks to explore the multi-stakeholders' perspectives on the implementation and use of learning analytics and understand the impact of learning analytics on learning and teaching.

 

## Opportunities Section ##

${_('Opportunities')}

At this point in our research we are seeking lecturers to participate along with the students enrolled in their courses.

There are two steps in seeking participants. First the lecturer is willing to participate and involve a course s/he is teaching. Second, students in the lecturer's course will be invited to participate on an entirely volunteer basis.
